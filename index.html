Privacy Policy

These privacy policies apply to all applications, games, websapps, and virtual reality games developed by the companies huskyEstudioSoft, huskyEstudioGames, and huskyCodeAI.

Personal Data Collection:

In our applications and games, we do not collect important personal information such as names, phone numbers, contacts, email addresses, or passwords. We value and respect the privacy of our users, and we do not request or store this type of sensitive information.

Third-Party Services:

In some of our applications, we may use third-party services to enhance the user experience. In the specific case of the accessibility and microphone bot, no personal information is collected. However, it's important to note that we use the Google voice recognition system to provide this functionality. For more details on Google's privacy practices, please review their privacy policy at the following link: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en.

Ads on Android and iOS:

In our Android and iOS applications, we use certain services that require an internet connection to provide advertisements to our users. The following third-party services are used:

  • Unity Ads: You can find information about Unity Ads' privacy policy at the following link: http://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Chartboost: We recommend reviewing Chartboost's privacy policy at the following link: https://answers.chartboost.com/hc/en-us/articles/200780269-Privacy-Policy.
  • Applovin: For more information about Applovin's privacy policy, please visit: https://www.applovin.com/privacy.
  • Google Play services: If you would like to learn about the terms and privacy policies of Google Play services, you can refer to: https://developers.google.com/games/services/terms.

Unity 3D:

Regarding our games and virtual reality games, we utilize Unity Analytics. You can find more information about their privacy policy at: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

In all our practices, we strive to protect the privacy and security of our users. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policies, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are committed to transparency and are here to provide the necessary support.
